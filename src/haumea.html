Haumea

Connect to the Haumea computing knodes

You need a connection to the network of the Universität Rostock:
  • Be inside the network of the Universität Rostock
  • Use the Cisco VPN
Open terminal and type:
  • ssh haumea1.hpc.uni-rostock.de -l jt292

Filetransfer via scp

Get files from haumea to the pc
  • scp -r jt292@haumea1.hpc.uni-rostock.de:/home/jt292/[FILEPATH] /Users/[DESTINATIONPATH]
